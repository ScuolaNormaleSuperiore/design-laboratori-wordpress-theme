Social Media Policy
The Scuola Normale Superiore uses social media for institutional purposes, to increase transparency and awareness of its activities: it informs, communicates, listens, and publishes institutional content. The published content includes images, videos, press releases, service information, news related to the activities of Scuola Normale, as well as opportunities for citizen engagement and participation. Through its social media channels, Scuola Normale may share and amplify content and messages of public interest and utility published by other users. Where directly published by Scuola Normale, all content is released according to the open by default principle under the CC-BY 4.0 license. We process your personal data as outlined in the privacy policy of this site. The Scuola Normale is committed to managing communication and dialogue spaces within its profiles, constantly monitored, on various social media platforms, asking its users to respect some simple rules:
  • Everyone is asked to express their opinion with fairness, moderation, and respect.
  • On social media, each individual is responsible for the content they post and the opinions they express. Insults, vulgarities, offenses, threats, and, in general, discriminatory, violent, or defamatory behaviors will not be tolerated.
  • Published content must always respect the privacy of individuals. References to facts or details that are irrelevant to the public interest and infringe on the personal sphere of third parties should be avoided.
  • The relevance of topics to the activities of Scuola Normale is an essential requirement: these spaces cannot be used for purposes other than institutional ones.
  • Every discussion is tied to a specific topic: all participants are required to respect it.
  • No form of advertising, spam, or promotion of private interests or illegal activities will be tolerated.
  • Content that infringes copyright or the unauthorized use of registered trademarks is not allowed.
